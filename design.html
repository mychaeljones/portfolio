PDX Guitar Duo (2011)

I was asked to create a website for the Portland Guitar Duo. They were lacking some key graphical components, so I designed them a logo based around an acoustic guitar. They liked the logo so much that it is now a part of their branding. I created this logo using Adobe Photoshop, which I understand is not the best practice, but sometimes it gets the job done.

Check out their logo and their site at www.PDXGuitarDuo.com.

How to Build a Fire (2014)

I created the album art for Sun Kill Moon, the upcoming How to Build a Fire album. It is composed of 4 different photos that I took while I was traveling the west coast of the United States.

More information on the upcoming album and on the band How to Build a Fire can be found at www.H2BAF.com.

Portland Guitar Duo Recordings 1999 - 2007 (2011)

The Portland Guitar Duo asked me to design the cover and insert art for their Recording 1999 - 2007 album. I created all the art work (besides the photographs) using a mix of Adobe Photoshop, inDesign, and Illustrator. I edited photos in Photoshop, created vector graphics in Illustrator, and used inDesign to layout the all the content before pushing the final product off to the publishing house.

You can find the release on iTunes, Amazon, and CD Baby - http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/portlandguitarduo.
